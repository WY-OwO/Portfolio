About

Hello! My name is Wei-Yan (Jocelyn) Chiang (She/They). I am a 22-year-old fifth-year undergraduate of University of California: Santa Cruz, double majoring in Arts & Designs: Games and Playable Media (AGPM), and Business Management Economics (BME). I have more than 15 years of art experience, 7 years of digital art experience, 2 years of 2D animation experience and 1 year of 3D modeling experience. I've taken classes relating to my gaming major such as Game Design I - III (game production), Game Art Production, Character Creation for Video Games, Interactive Storytelling, and Introduction to Computer Programming for the Arts. I am proficient in both English and Mandarin.

I am a digital artist and animator who is very passionate about character design and am always excited to work from concepting to fully-rendered artworks or even 3D models. During my own time I love to come up with character designs based on aesthetics that I like or stories I develop on the side. I have commission experiences, and keep in active content with my commissioners to make sure I deliver exactly what they want.
