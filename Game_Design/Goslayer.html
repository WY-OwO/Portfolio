Capstone Project: Godslayer

For my capstone game project (Game Design II and III), I have teamed up with a handful of talented artists, writers and coders to make Godslayer. Godslayer is a turn-base time loop RPG featuring four playable characters fighting the final boss of their adventure to save their home. The goal is to strategically use the unlocked player skills to defeat the final boss five times. There would be character lore and relations revealed throughout the game loop that would lead to the discovery that the blurred final boss was the boss the team once fought in a previous adventure, and had fused with their passed
