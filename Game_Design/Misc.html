ARTG170 Capstone Game - Go With The Flow

Play the game on itch.io!

For one of my capstone game projects, I joined a team of wonderful artists, writers and programmer to create the game "Go With The Flow". Following the prompt "Collage: 'Verse Jumping", we have decided to make a runner game about our main character, Beanie, chasing after his friend, Flow, through three different dimensions. With each dimension, there's a change in the reason why Beanie chases after Flow. Their relationship develops from playful to protecting, to a happy marriage. Or so it was intended to be. Due to time constraint, the game is unpolished.

I have contributed the character concept of Beanie and Flow, dimension 2's character portraits, both dimensions 1 and 2's animation, and dimension 2's monster design.

ARTG170 Capstone Game - Hellmouth

Play the game on itch.io!
For the third and final game project for this capstone game design class, the team came together once again to make a visual novel about four university students exploring a cave named 'Hell Mouth' for fun, only to discover how unsettling the cave becomes the deeper they explore, until they turn back to face each other again and tries to leave, and hopefully with the versions of them they came in with.
