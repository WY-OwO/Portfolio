GDA Games Contribution

Game Design & Art Collaboration, or GDA, is a game design club in University of California, Santa Cruz. The club is divided into departments based on what's needd in a typical game studio (arts, programming, music, etc.), but often comes together for workshops that teaches valuable technical skills such as how to connect to the GDA Github or how to navigate Unity (the game engine we commonly use for mock studio game development).

Each academic year, there will be students pitching the ideas of their proposed game, and all the club members will vote on what game they are interested in the most in fall quarter to work on for both winter and spring quarter. We will develop the chosen game in a mock studio format throughout roughly 20 weeks time and present the final game in an open studio.
