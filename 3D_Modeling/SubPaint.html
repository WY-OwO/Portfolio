Adobe Substance Painter/Designer

Here are some work I've done in Adobe Substance Painter, Substance Designer and Substance Sampler. I took a course during my third year in UCSC and here is everything I've made (in no chronological order).

MeetMat Momo

I really liked Momo from the game Stray (2022), and thought it would be an interesting choice to transform Momo onto a MeetMat. I sketched out my idea and pinpointed what needs to be modeled separately in Blender (Hat, hat strap, chin wirings).

Jacket Design

I've learned that meshes are capable of being textured with transparency, so I took this chance of designing a jacket to play with the transparency and make a breezy mesh jacket. I was also inspired by one of my favorite places I've visited: Dun Huang, China, and decide to make that my logo's theme, of a flower that's found on one of the many wall paintings in the Long-Men Grotto.

Old and New Scales

Two scales, one brand new, and the other old. This practice is to familiarize myself with thinking about how wear-n-tears should be placed sensibly, instead of scattered everywhere. I considered how a scale would be used and what materials on the scale would be worn (scale plate, the top surface of the scale body, edges of the scale display, a side replacement).
